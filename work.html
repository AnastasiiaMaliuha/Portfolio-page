This is my first Asap Barbershop project. I started it in 2018. After talking to some of the men in our city, it turned out that they had nowhere to get their hair and beard in order. We also wanted to create a cozy atmosphere, where you can come and relax.
After two years in business, we realized that we needed to expand and improve. We decided to move to a bigger space. Since all of us and our clients were coffee lovers, next door in a vacant space there was an enthusiasm to make a coffee shop, which would work separately, but at the same time treat customers barbershop fresh coffee.The coffee shop was named Lungo, after one of the drinks.
